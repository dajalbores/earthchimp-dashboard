Naturally tasty flavor/no aftertaste
Aside from the exorbitant price for such an underwhelming amount of product, the shake really was pretty awesome. There was a disclaimer on back about the "texture," but it was almost nonexistent like the chalky texture of Vega or other supposed comparable protein powders. The taste was actually good- not just edible but actually desirable for a flavor. (I got vanilla, btw) I didn't even get any real after taste, either, which was the best part.
